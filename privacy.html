Privacy Policy

🔒 Last updated: June 12, 2026 🌍 GDPR & CCPA compliant 🛡️ Solvique Atelier

1. Introduction & Our Commitment

At Solvique, your privacy is not an afterthought — it is foundational to how we operate. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website solvique.com, make a purchase, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with us in any way. We are committed to transparency, data minimalism, and giving you meaningful control over your information.

By using the Site, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Site. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a seamless shopping experience and to improve our products and services. This includes:

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account & Order Data: Name, email address, shipping/billing address, phone number, payment information (processed securely via PCI-compliant partners).
  • Communications: Any correspondence you send to us via email, chat, or contact forms.
  • Preferences: Size guides, wishlist items, product reviews, and marketing preferences.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, time spent, and clickstream data.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use essential, functional, and analytics cookies (see Section 5 for details).

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from payment processors (to confirm transactions), shipping carriers (for delivery updates), or social media platforms when you interact with our content.

📌 Data minimalism pledge: Solvique never sells your personal data. We never share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your trust is our most valuable asset.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order fulfillment: Processing payments, shipping products, providing order confirmations and tracking.
  • Customer service: Responding to inquiries, processing returns and exchanges, resolving issues.
  • Improving our Site & products: Analyzing usage trends, personalizing your experience, developing new collections.
  • Marketing (with your consent): Sending newsletters, exclusive offers, and lookbooks if you have opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Legal compliance: Detecting fraud, enforcing our Terms of Service, complying with applicable laws.

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that significantly affects you without human oversight.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and remember your preferences. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

5. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal data. We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help us operate (payment gateways, shipping carriers, email platforms, IT hosting). They are contractually bound to use data only for specified services.
  • Legal obligations: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety (or that of others).
  • Business transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, your data may be transferred as part of the assets, with notice provided.

We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, firewalls, access controls, and regular security audits. Payment information is tokenized and processed by PCI DSS Level 1 certified partners — we never store full credit card details on our servers.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example:

  • Order data: 7 years (for tax and warranty purposes).
  • Account information: Until you request deletion or account inactivity for 5+ years.
  • Newsletter data: Until you unsubscribe.

8. Children's Privacy

Solvique does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to external websites (e.g., Instagram, payment providers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. International Data Transfers

Solvique is based in France, but we serve customers worldwide. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. For transfers outside the European Economic Area, we ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place to protect your data.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes via email (if you have an account) or a prominent notice on our Site. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.
