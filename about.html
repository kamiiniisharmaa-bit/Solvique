Solvique was born from a quiet rebellion against fast fashion — a commitment to pieces that earn their place in your life through integrity, craft, and time.

A different kind of house.

Slow. Intentional. Radical in its quietness.

Solvique began in 2018 as a small atelier in the heart of Paris, driven by a simple belief: clothing should not demand to be replaced every season. We design for the long arc — pieces that move with you, that soften and improve with age, that carry memory and meaning.

Every Solvique garment is the result of months, sometimes years, of material research. We work exclusively with mills that share our ethics: organic fibers, closed-loop dyeing, transparent wages. We reject the conventional fashion calendar, releasing collections only when they are truly ready.

What we stand for

Four principles that guide every stitch, every decision.

🌾

Material Honesty

We source linen, wool, and cotton from regenerative farms. No polyester blends, no greenwashing — only traceable, natural fibers.

🕰️

Radical Durability

Every seam is reinforced. Every button is ethically sourced. We offer a lifetime mending service — because repair is an act of love.

🤝

Transparent Atelier

Our workshops in Portugal and France pay living wages. We publish our supply chain openly and invite audits from our community.

🌍

Carbon-Conscious

We ship plastic-free, offset remaining emissions, and are working toward circularity with a take-back program launching 2027.
